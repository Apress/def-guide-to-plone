This is a collection of examples from the Plone book.

Installing:

- Copy the whole directory containing this file into the Products directory of your Plone instance

- Restart your Plone.

- Log in to your Plone site and go to portal setup > Add / Remove Products

- Then click on install WordExample

This is the procedure documented in Chapter 10 of the book.

To do transformations use PortalTransformations as described in Chapter 14 of the book. This will require installation or setup of third party transformation tools such as wvware, MS Office and Python Win 32, or Open Office and PyUNO.
